Algorithm - Chores

1. List out amount of time each chore will take, figure out amount of time available to actually do chores
2. Prioritize chores into a list organized from shortest to longest.
3. If total time for chores is greater than time available, add shortest chores to list of chores to accomplish within time constraints. Else, add all chores to list.
3. Complete a chore, check list for next chore
4. If chore, repeat step 3.
5. After relax
